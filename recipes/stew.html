Classic beef stew

Description

Beef stew is a lifetime favorite of mine, though I've never made it myself. It's a perfect meal at the end of a long, cold, windy day. The combination of onion, bell pepper, and celery always creates a hearty flavor to die for, and adding carrots and potatoes just takes this thing straight to flavor town!

This is also great with something like cornbread or dinner rolls on the side, and I sometimes add a little extra diced onion for a bit of a crunch when I make a bowl for myself.

Ingredients

  • Boneless beef chuck
  • Olive oil
  • Carrots
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Celery
  • Minced garlic
  • Flour
  • Beef stock
  • Yukon gold potatoes
  • Spices/seasoning
